Research Interests

Learning and decision-making in complex environments:

  • Generative models and synthetic data;

  • Integrative data analysis: multi-task learning, transfer learning, non-stationary online learning;

  • Latent variable models and representation learning;

  • Stochastic optimization and non-convex optimization.
